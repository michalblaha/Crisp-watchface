# Crisp — appstore release notes

Short text for the "release notes" field when publishing a version on the
Pebble / Rebble appstore. See `../CHANGELOG.md` for the full history.

## v1.1.0

Now called Crisp, with live info in every corner:

- New: four configurable corners - battery, Bluetooth, heart rate, steps,
  weather (today's low/high) or rain chance.
- New: weather and rain chance via Open-Meteo and your phone's location.
- New: temperature in Celsius, Fahrenheit, or automatic (follows your watch's
  units).
- Second hand now off by default; tweak everything from the built-in config.

## v1.0.0

First release. A clean analog watchface that scales to every Pebble: thin
markers, optional second hand and date, battery on the hour markers, a
disconnection indicator, and full color customization.
