# Crisp — Pebble appstore listing copy

## Title

Crisp

## Tagline (one line)

Clean analog watchface with thin markers and live info in every corner.

## Short description (~140 chars)

A customizable analog watchface: thin markers, an optional second hand, recolorable hands, and four corners you fill with the data you care about.

## Full description

Crisp is a clean analog watchface that scales perfectly to every Pebble — from
the classic 144x168 screens through the Pebble Time 2 (200x228) to the round
Pebble models. Thin markers ring the dial, the current five-minute sector lights
up its minute ticks, and you decide exactly what is shown and what fills the
four corners.

Make it yours:

- Four configurable corners. Each corner shows one of: battery level, Bluetooth
  status, heart rate, step count, today's weather (low/high temperature), or the
  current rain chance — or leave any corner empty.
- Temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit, or automatic to match your watch's
  metric/imperial setting.
- Optional second hand, off by default to save battery.
- Optional date block: weekday, day of the month and month.
- A battery gauge drawn straight onto the hour markers.
- Recolor every element: hour markers, minute markers, charging markers, the
  hour and minute hands, the hand tips and center, the second hand and its tip,
  and the day of the month.
- Disconnection indicator: the center cap hollows out when your phone is out of
  range.
- A smooth sweep animation when the face loads.

Weather and rain chance use your phone's location and the free Open-Meteo
service, so those two corners need the Pebble app connected. Everything else
runs entirely on the watch — your face stays fully configured even without your
phone.

Configuration is built in via Clay; no website or account is required.
